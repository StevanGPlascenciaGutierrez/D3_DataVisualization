Regional Video Game Sales
Hover over area to focus

Video Games weren't always so popular.
They had a slow start in the 80s and only in the 2000s did they really become such a big deal. Games produced in the late 2000s have sold the most copies. This could be due to their long time on the market so recent games can be expected to be more in the future.
In Japan, games released in all years have sold well
In the European Union, games made in the 2000s sold well
In North America, games released in all years have sold well
In Other Regions, games released in the late 2000s sold well.
